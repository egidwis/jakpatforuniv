How to Roll Back to a Previous Deployment in Cloudflare Pages

Step 1: Log in to Cloudflare Dashboard

Go to https://dash.cloudflare.com/ and log in with your credentials.

Step 2: Navigate to Pages

From the left sidebar, click on Workers & Pages.

Step 3: Select Your Project

Find and click on the jakpatforuniv-submit project.

Step 4: Go to Deployments Tab

Click on the Deployments tab to see all your deployments.
